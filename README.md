# music_quiz
A test to see if I can make a quiz that people can play


Aim:

Make a quiz that gives you an image of a country flag and the first 1 second of a song (probably Beatles or Oasis) and then you have to guess the country and the song  

For less points you can get helpful hints / facts about the country and a longer sample from the song  

5 / 10 / 15 questions depending on chosen difficulty and then show points and ask if they want to try again
